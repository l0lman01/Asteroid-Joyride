Asteroid Joyride

Navigate the void. Collect coins. Survive.

How to Play

🚀 SPACE

Press Space to thrust upward and dodge obstacles. Gravity pulls you down, so time your jumps carefully.

💥 CLICK

Click anywhere to fire a shot. Destroy incoming asteroids before they reach you.

🪙 COINS

Collect coins to boost your score. Every 50 points the game speeds up, so stay sharp.

🔄 RESTART

Hit the restart button in the top-left corner after a crash to try again and beat your high score.

Pro Tips

  • Keep moving — staying in one place makes you an easy target for asteroid clusters
  • Shoot strategically; focus on asteroids directly in your flight path
  • Coins appear in groups of 10 — plan your path to grab the whole line
  • The game gets faster over time, so build your score early while the pace is manageable
  • Your high score persists during the session — keep playing to beat your personal best
